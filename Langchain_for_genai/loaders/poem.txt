**The Spirit of Cricket**

On the green field, dreams take flight,
Bats swing boldly in the morning light.
The bowler runs with fearless pace,
Determined to win with skill and grace.

The ball flies high into the sky,
Crowds erupt with every cry.
A perfect cover drive, so smooth and neat,
Turns every heartbeat into a thrilling beat.

Fielders dive with courage and pride,
Never letting hope or spirit hide.
Every wicket tells a story untold,
Of passion, patience, and hearts of gold.

Victory smiles on those who believe,
Even after moments that make them grieve.
Cricket teaches teamwork and trust,
To rise again is always a must.

From streets to grand stadium lights,
It fills our days and endless nights.
More than a game, it's a way of life,
Bringing joy through every challenge and strife.
With every match, new legends are born,
And the love for cricket lives on forevermore. 🏏
