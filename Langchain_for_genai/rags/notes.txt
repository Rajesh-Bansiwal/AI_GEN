**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** ek technique hai jisme pehle **Indexing** ke through external documents (jaise PDFs, websites, ya files) 
ko chunks me divide karke unke embeddings bana kar Vector Database me store kiya jata hai taaki woh searchable ban sake.
 Jab user koi question poochta hai, to **Retrieval** step me us question se related sabse relevant chunks Vector Database se dhoondhe jaate hain. 
 Iske baad **Augmentation** me in retrieved chunks ko user ke original question ke saath combine karke ek complete prompt banaya jata hai. 
 Aakhir me **Generation** step me ye augmented prompt LLM ko diya jata hai, jo apni knowledge aur diye gaye context dono ka use karke accurate aur context-aware 
 final answer generate karta hai.
whaty is tokens
A token is the smallest unit of text that an LLM reads and processes. Instead of reading complete sentences or words, an 
LLM first breaks the input into tokens and then processes those tokens.
